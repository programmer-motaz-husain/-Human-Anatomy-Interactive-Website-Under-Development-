💧 The Lymphatic System

The lymphatic system 💧 is an important part of the body’s
defense system 🛡️. It helps protect us from infections 🦠
and keeps body fluids balanced ⚖️. It carries a clear fluid
called lymph 💧, which contains white blood cells ⚪ that
fight germs and diseases.

This system includes lymph nodes, vessels, and organs like
the spleen. Lymph nodes act like filters 🧽, trapping
bacteria and viruses 🦠 before they can spread in the body.

The lymphatic system also helps remove waste and excess
fluids 🗑️ from tissues and returns them to the bloodstream ❤️.

It plays a key role in keeping the body healthy, clean,
and protected 💚⚔️.
