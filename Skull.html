💀 The Skull

The skull 💀 is a strong structure made of bones 🦴 that
protects the brain 🧠 from injury ⚠️. It acts like a hard
shield 🛡️, keeping one of the most important organs safe at
all times.

It also gives shape to the face 🙂 and supports important
sensory organs like the eyes 👀, nose 👃, and ears 👂,
helping us see 👁️, smell 🌸, and hear 🔊 the world around us.

The skull is made up of several bones 🧩 joined together,
forming a solid structure that does not move 🚫. These bones
are designed to be both strong 💪 and slightly flexible to
absorb shocks.

In addition, the skull protects delicate structures inside
the head, such as blood vessels 🩸 and nerves ⚡, which are
essential for brain function and communication throughout
the body.
