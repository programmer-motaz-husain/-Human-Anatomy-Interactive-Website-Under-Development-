⚡ The Nerves

Nerves ⚡ are like the body’s electrical wiring 🔌.
They are bundles of fibers that carry signals between the
brain 🧠, spinal cord, and the rest of the body 🏃.
These signals allow the body to feel sensations 👋, move
muscles 💪, and react to the environment 🌳 quickly.

    There are three main types of nerves:
  1. Sensory nerves 👁️ – carry information from the senses (like touch, sight, smell) to the brain 🧠.
  2. Motor nerves 🏃 – send commands from the brain to muscles 💪 to control movement.
  3. Autonomic nerves 💓 – control involuntary functions like heartbeat ❤️, digestion 🍽️, and breathing 🫁.
Nerves work with the brain and spinal cord 🧠 to form the
nervous system, which is essential for coordinating all body
activities ⚡ and keeping us safe and responsive 🛡️.
